			<h1 class="title">Spanish police rescue Nigerian girls sold for sex after voodoo threats</h1>
				<div class="post-meta">
		<p class="meta-info">
			Posted  by <a href="http://www.thekenyatoday.com/author/Kenya%20Today/" title="Posts by Kenya Today" rel="author">Kenya Today</a> on Mar 22, 2018 in <a href="http://www.thekenyatoday.com/category/africa/" rel="category tag">Africa</a> | <a href="http://www.thekenyatoday.com/africa/2018/03/spanish-police-rescue-nigerian-girls-sold-for-sex-after-voodoo-threats/#respond">0 comments</a>		</p>
	</div> <!-- end .post-meta -->

							
									<div class="thumb">
						<img src="https://i1.wp.com/www.thekenyatoday.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/87508676_police2.jpg?resize=200%2C200" class='single-thumb'  alt='Spanish police rescue Nigerian girls sold for sex after voodoo threats' width='200' height='200' />						<span class="overlay"></span>
					</div> 	<!-- end .thumb -->
							
			<p><strong>Reuters</strong></p>
<p>Spanish police have rescued 39 Nigerian girls and women from a notorious sex trafficking ring who kept them in squalid cave-like houses and forced them into prostitution after threatening them with black magic, Europol announced on Thursday.</p><div class='code-block code-block-4' style='margin: 8px auto; text-align: center; clear: both;'>
<script async src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js"></script>
<!-- Large Rectangle -->
<ins class="adsbygoogle"
     style="display:inline-block;width:336px;height:280px"
     data-ad-client="ca-pub-7869198785501651"
     data-ad-slot="2749704123"></ins>
<script>
(adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
</script></div>

<p>The operation, carried out with British and Nigerian law enforcement agencies, led to the arrests of 89 people including a well-known Nigerian DJ, according to a Europol statement.</p>
<p>It said the Nigerian crime ring was linked to the Eiye Confraternity &#8211; a network that Spanish police have previously compared to the mafioso in Chicago in the 1930s.</p><div class='code-block code-block-3' style='margin: 8px 0; clear: both;'>
<strong><a href="https://www.trendiee.com" target="_blank">Do you something awesome to share with the world? Click here to share</a></strong></div>

<p>The victims, many under the age of 18, were believed to have undergone voodoo &#8220;juju&#8221; rituals in Nigeria to force them to comply with orders, Britain&#8217;s National Crime Agency (NCA) said.</p>
<p>After being trafficked to Spain via Libya and Italy, the women and girls were kept in cave houses in the southeastern city of Almeria, it said.</p>
<p>They were then forced into prostitution to pay off &#8220;debts&#8221; of 30,000 euros ($36,900) each that they were told they owed the organisation for their journey to Europe.</p><div class='code-block code-block-1' style='margin: 8px auto; text-align: center; clear: both;'>
<script async src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js"></script>
<!-- Responsive 1 -->
<ins class="adsbygoogle"
     style="display:block"
     data-ad-client="ca-pub-7869198785501651"
     data-ad-slot="1526348015"
     data-ad-format="auto"></ins>
<script>
(adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
</script></div>

<p>Europol said the DJ had helped transfer the victims to Spain and &#8220;organise sexual exploitation&#8221; in several provinces.</p>
<p>It said bank accounts used by the network to launder more than 300,000 euros had been blocked.</p>
<p>&#8220;(This) was a highly organised crime gang, exploiting young woman for lengthy periods of time, keeping them in horrendous conditions where they knew there would be no escape,&#8221; the NCA&#8217;s deputy director Tom Dowdall said in a statement.</p>
<p>Europol said raids were carried out across 11 Spanish cities last November but the operation could not be announced until Thursday to avoid jeopardising investigations.</p>
<p>The NCA said police had also arrested a Nigerian woman in Manchester this year who they believe was controlling some of the victims in Spain. She is in custody awaiting extradition to Spain, it said.</p><div class='code-block code-block-5' style='margin: 8px auto; text-align: center; clear: both;'>
<script async src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js"></script>

<ins class="adsbygoogle"
     style="display:block"
     data-ad-client="ca-pub-7869198785501651"
     data-ad-slot="1526348015"
     data-ad-format="auto"></ins>
<script>

</script></div>

<p>Europol said Eiye operated in clandestine groups all over the world, funding the brotherhood in Nigeria through both legal and illicit activities, particularly through human trafficking.</p>
