On Facebook, more than 2,000 counterfeit Tiffany listings have been taken down since the beginning of 2017," she told Business Insider. </p><h2> Sellers asked for bank transfers, phone numbers, and for money via an unprotected PayPal feature </h2><p> The most alarming aspect of Facebook Marketplace was how unsafe it was for a buyer. </p><p> Despite Facebook's safety tips, there's little advice on the best way to pay, or how to keep your information safe. The Facebook Marketplace tab also does not directly link to Facebook's help pages.

Paying through Friends and Family waives that protection &mdash; meaning unscrupulous sellers could take your money and never post you anything. </p><p> A PayPal spokeswoman confirmed that Friends and Family payments do not come with buyer protection and explicitly warned against buying goods and services through the mechanism.
