<p><span>&nbsp;</span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span>Alfie's medical condition causes him to have up to 150 seizures per month.&nbsp; &nbsp;His parents recently took him to the Netherlands to receive treatment with cannabis oil and whilst receiving treatment Alfie seizures&nbsp;reduced to one per month.</span></p>
<p dir="ltr"></p>
