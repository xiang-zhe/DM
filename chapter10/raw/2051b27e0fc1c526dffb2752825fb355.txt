<p>According to the report, published Wednesday, Nix referred to two potential clients, both of whom were black, as &#8220;n&#8212;&#8211;s&#8221;; neither the identities of the individuals nor the recipients of the email were disclosed.</p>
<p>Nix was suspended on Tuesday after <a href="https://www.thewrap.com/cambridge-analytica-ceo-brags-about-brag-about-entrapping-politicians-with-bribes-and-sex-workers/">Britain&#8217;s Channel 4 published a video</a> in which he was seen bragging to an undercover reporter that Cambridge Analytica has used bribes and sex workers to entrap politicians on behalf of the company&#8217;s clients.</p>
