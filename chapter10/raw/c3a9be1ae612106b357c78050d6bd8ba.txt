    <h1 class="post-title">Mahmoud Abbas blames Hamas for Gaza attack on PM's convoy</h1>
    <p class="article-heading-des">President Abbas says Hamas orchestrated attack on prime minister's convoy, prompting Hamas to call for elections. </p>
    <div class="article-heading-author-wrap">
      <div class="article-heading-author-img">
        <div class="article-heading-author-name">
          <div class="article-duration"><time class="timeagofunction" datetime="Tue Mar 20 2018 08:32:09 GMT+0000">20 Mar 2018 08:32 GMT</time></div>
        </div>

    <div class="article-readToMe-share-block" id="article-top-share-icon">

</div></div></div></div></div></div><div></div></div></div></div><div><div><div><div style="width:100%"><div><div class="row article-main-content-wrapper"><div><div><div><div style="width:100%"><div><div class="col-md-8 article-main-content"><div><div style=";border-style:none"><article id="main-article-block" class="main-article-block clear-fix"><div class="main-article-body"><figure class="main-article-mediaCaption"><div class="main-article-media"><img class="img-responsive main-article-media-img" src="/mritems/imagecache/mbdxxlarge/mritems/Images/2018/1/14/a80e790f04aa4b94af0f68f36d090c51_18.jpg" alt="Mahmoud Abbas said that if the &quot;assassination attempt&quot; had succeeded, the attack would have opened the door for a bloody civil war [File: Mohamad Torokman/Reuters]" title="Mahmoud Abbas blames Hamas for Gaza attack on PM's convoy"></div><figcaption class="main-article-caption">Mahmoud Abbas said that if the "assassination attempt" had succeeded, the attack would have opened the door for a bloody civil war [File: Mohamad Torokman/Reuters]</figcaption></figure><div id="inpage-more-on-block" class="inpage-auto-embed hidden-xs"></div>
    <div class="article-p-wrapper" id="body-200771816342556199"><p class="speakable">President of the Palestinian Authority, <a href="https://www.aljazeera.com/focus/2009/11/2009115154854751132.html">Mahmoud Abbas</a>, has accused Hamas of orchestrating the <a class="InternalLink" href="https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2018/03/explosion-hits-palestinian-pm-rami-hamdallah-enters-gaza-180313083301418.html">explosion</a> that targeted the convoy of Prime Minister Rami Hamdallah as he entered the Gaza Strip last week.Â </p>
<p class="speakable">"We do not want them to investigate, we do not want information from them, we do not want anything from them because we know exactly that they, the Hamas movement, were the ones who committed this incident," Abbas said at a meeting of the Palestinian leadership in Ramallah late on Monday.Â </p>
<p class="speakable">Hamdallah's convoy, which included the Palestinian Authority's intelligence chief Majed Faraj, was attacked just after the delegation crossed through the Israeli-controlled Erez checkpoint, known to Palestinians as Beit Hanoun, in northern <a href="https://www.aljazeera.com/topics/city/gaza.html">Gaza</a>.Â </p>
<div class="inpage-embed article-guid" data-guid="171012115017367">
<div style="display: none;">{articleGUID}</div>
</div>
<p>Faraj and Hamdallah remained unharmed, while seven security guards were wounded in the blast.Â </p>
<p><span>Shortly after the attack, Hamas said it was launching an investigation to uncover who was behind the blast and deflected the PA's comments blaming the Gaza-based group for the incident.Â </span></p>
<p>Speaking during Monday's meeting, Abbas said that if the "assassination attempt" had succeeded, the development would have opened the door for a bloody civil war.Â </p>
<h2>Call for elections</h2>
<p>In response to Abbas' accusations, Hamas called for elections.Â </p>
<p>"We are shocked by the tense stance that Abbas has taken. This position burns bridges and strengthens division and strikes the unity of our people," Hamas said in a press release.Â </p>
<div class="inpage-embed article-guid" data-guid="171012064342008">
<div style="display: none;">{articleGUID}</div>
</div>
<p>"In light of all this, Hamas calls for general elections, including presidential, parliamentary and national council elections, so that the Palestinian people can choose their leadership."</p>
<p>The attack and subsequent statements by both sides mark a serious deterioration in relations between Hamas and the Palestinian Authority, the semi-autonomous body that governs the occupied West Bank.Â </p>
<p><a href="https://www.aljazeera.com/topics/organisations/fatah.html">Fatah</a>, the ruling party within the PA, and <a href="https://www.aljazeera.com/topics/organisations/hamas.html">Hamas</a>, the party that governs the occupied Gaza Strip, signed a reconciliation agreement in October 2017, ending a decade of division that saw two parallel governments operating in Gaza and the West Bank, respectively.</p>
<p>But the deal was never fully implemented due to differences within the two political factions, which are the largest in Palestinian politics.Â </p>
<p>Analysts <a class="InternalLink" href="https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2018/03/explosion-hits-palestinian-pm-rami-hamdallah-enters-gaza-180313083301418.html">said</a> the attack on Hamdallah's convoy was intended to put a strain on reconciliation efforts. Â </p></div>
