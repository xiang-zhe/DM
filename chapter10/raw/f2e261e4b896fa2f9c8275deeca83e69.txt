<br>The minister was responding to a question on whether the ISRO has started working on building igloo-like habitats on the lunar surface for potential future missions. 
<br>
<br>An <a href="/topic/igloo" target="_blank">igloo</a> is a shelter, a place for people to stay warm and dry made from blocks of snow placed on top of each other. 
<br>
<br>"Various options are being studied about the requirements and complexities of habitats. The study is more towards futuristic developments," Singh said. 
<br>
<br>ISRO had first launched its <a href="/topic/Moon" target="_blank">Moon</a> mission <a href="/topic/Chandrayaan-1" target="_blank">Chandrayaan-1</a> in 2008. In its second mission - the Chandrayaan-2 - a rover will be made to land on the moon's yet-unexplored south pole. 
<br>
<br>The rover will send high-quality pictures that will help in better understanding the moon.
