SEOUL, March 20 (Yonhap) -- North Korean leader Kim Jong-un on Tuesday extended his "heartfelt congratulations" to Russian President Vladimir Putin for his landslide re-election victory.
   "Your reelection as president is an expression of your people's great support and trust in you," Kim said in a message carried by North Korea's official Korean Central News Agency.
   Kim also said he is convinced that the bilateral friendship and cooperation would continue to develop and deepen in line with the desire and wishes of the peoples of the two countries.
